Pinnacle Research

Pinnacle Research is a student-led quantitative finance project analyzing public companies using data, mathematics, and Python.

Research

  • Apple (AAPL) — Stock performance & risk analysis
  • Tesla (TSLA) — Volatility and trend analysis
  • Starbucks (SBUX) — Comparative stock analysis

Methods

Analysis is conducted using Python with pandas and NumPy to study historical stock price data, calculate returns, and visualize trends.

About

Founded by high school students interested in finance, mathematics, and computer science.
